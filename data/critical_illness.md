# ❤️ Critical Illness Shield

## Overview

Critical Illness Shield pays a lump sum upon diagnosis of major illnesses like cancer, heart attack, or stroke.

## Key Details

- **Category:** Critical Illness
- **Coverage Amount:** $75,000
- **Monthly Premium:** $28.00
- **Deductible:** $0
- **Plan Code:** CIS-75
- **Status:** Active
- **Effective Date:** 2024-01-01
- **Eligibility:** Ages 18–65

## Included Features

- Lump-sum payout at diagnosis
- Coverage for rare conditions (fictional expansion)
- $1,000 Home Support Boost
- $200 Early Detection Reimbursement option

## Exclusions

- Pre-existing conditions within 6 months
- Illness from experimental gene-editing devices
- Cosmetic complications

## Example Scenario

A beneficiary is diagnosed with cancer and receives the $75,000 payout plus a $1,000 home-care boost.
