# ♿ Service-Disabled Veterans Insurance (S-DVI)

## Overview

S-DVI provides life insurance for veterans with service-connected disabilities.

## Key Details

- **Category:** Life Insurance
- **Coverage Amount:** $10,000
- **Monthly Premium:** $8.00
- **Deductible:** $0
- **Plan Code:** SDVI-10
- **Status:** Active
- **Effective Date:** 2024-01-01
- **Eligibility:** Ages 18–70, disability rating required

## Included Features

- Guaranteed acceptance
- Premium waiver for total disability
- $50 annual "Wellness Reward" for VA checkups

## Exclusions

- Paid extreme combat simulation events
- Non-prescribed controlled substance-related deaths

## Example Scenario

A veteran with a 30% disability rating enrolls and later qualifies for a full premium waiver.
