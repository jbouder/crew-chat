# ➕ Supplemental Term Life

## Overview

Supplemental Term Life provides additional life insurance coverage to meet higher financial obligations.

## Key Details

- **Category:** Supplemental Life
- **Coverage Amount:** $500,000
- **Monthly Premium:** $55.00
- **Deductible:** $0
- **Plan Code:** STL-500
- **Status:** Active
- **Effective Date:** 2024-01-01
- **Eligibility:** Ages 21–55

## Included Features

- Portable term life coverage
- Child and spouse riders up to $50k
- Guaranteed renewal
- Annual financial planning consultation

## Exclusions

- Death from illegal stunt competitions
- Fraudulent estate documents
- Outdated or missing beneficiary records

## Example Scenario

A service member adds Supplemental Term Life coverage after expanding family responsibilities.
